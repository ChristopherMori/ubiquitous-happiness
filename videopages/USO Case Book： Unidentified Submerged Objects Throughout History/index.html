    USO Case Book: Unidentified Submerged Objects Throughout History¶

    Documentary-style overview of Unidentified Submerged Objects (USOs): historical “maritime lightwheels,” mid-20th-century merchant-ship encounters logged by Blue Book, and Soviet/Russian naval case files. Emphasizes transmedium behavior (seamless air-sea transitions), extreme underwater speeds without cavitation, bioluminescent effects, and radar/sonar anomalies. Includes notable merchant, military, and scientific witnesses across the Persian Gulf, South China Sea, Atlantic and Pacific, Barents Sea, and Australian waters.
